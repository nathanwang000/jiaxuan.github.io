I'm a rising third year computer science and engineering PhD student at the Unversity of Michigan, adviced by professor Jenna Wiens.

My research involves developing new algorithms to work with time series data, usually with healthcare motivation. I've done research in non convex optimization, model interpretability, and temporal conditional shift. I've also had experience applying reinforcement learning techniques to basketball analytics.

I spent most of my pre college years in Beijing. Then, I spent 6 years in Ann Arbor at the University of Michigan, finishing my undergrad as a computer science major and math minor in 2017, and staying here as a PhD student. During my undergrad years, I worked with professor Jia Deng to augment CNN with rotation invariant filters (2015-2016). I aslo worked as a machine learning intern at Bloomberg L.P. (2016) on parsing and ranking natural language query for financial charts, under the supervision of Dr. Konstantine Arkoudas and Dr. Srivas Prasad.
